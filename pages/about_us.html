Who are we?

We are Inaudible. A website dedicated to bring back the old tradition of reading books instead of listening to some random person reading books for you.

Ok... But why?

The advent of Tik Tok and short form videos have destroyed people's attention span. So much so that it is on par with a gold fish no cap.

Which is why this website was born. We aim to increase this generation's crappy attention span by prividing them interesting stories that they could read.
